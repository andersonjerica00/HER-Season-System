HER SEASON : A Self empowerment System for single mothers. 
PROJECT OVERVIEW: HER Season is a self empowerment system designed for single mothers who want to prioritize themselves while still thriving in motherhood. It focuses onself care, health, confidence, identity, and personal growth through simple structured habits. This project demonstrates product thinking by turning a real life emotional and lifestyle challenge into a simple system that supports behavior change and long term personal growth.
PROBLEM: Many single mothers spend the most of their time focused on responsibilties and caregiving while neglacting their own health, identity, and personal development. Over time, this can lead to burnout, low confidence, and loss of self identity. The core challenge is not motivation but lack of structure that makes self care and personal growth feel realistic and sustainable.
USER: Single mothers want to: -Rebuild confidence and identity -Improve self care and health habits -Create better daily routines   -Spend intentional quailty time with their children -Grow personally and spiritually over time.
SOLUTION: HER Season provides a structured but flexible lifestyle system that helps users focus on:                                 - Self care routines including skin, hair, nails, hygiene, and rest. -Health habits such as movement and nutrition awareness.       - Confidence building through refelction and intentional self presentation. - Motherhood connection through quality time and shared experiences. - Personal growth through goal setting and mindset development. (The system breaks overwhelming life goals into small consistent actions).
PRODUCT STRUCTURE: -Daily Self Check                                                                                                 "Did I care for my body today?" "Did I care for my mind today?" "Did I do something for my confidence today?"
WEEKLY FOCUS: -Self care routines - Health and wellness habits -Confidence and identity practices -Quality time with children
MONTHLY GROWTH FOCUS: -Personal goals - Skill development - Financial awareness and mindset growth
PRODUCT THINKING APPROACH: This system was designed with a ficus on simplicity, emotional wellbeing, and behavior change.            Key Principles: - Reduce overwhelm by breaking goals into small actions. - Build consistency instead of perfection.               - Support emotional wellness and identity rebuilding. - Make self care realistic for busy mothers. -Design for long term lifestyle change instead of short term motivation.
FUTURE VISION: - Mobile app with reminders amd habit tracking - AI guided personal coaching and encouragement                      - Community space for single mothers - Travel and experience planning tools for moothers and children                              - Financial and skill development pathways
TECH USED: Notion for prototype system design -Product thinking and system design principles - AI assisted ideation and structuring.
PURPOSE OF THIS PROJECT: This prjoecr demonstrates product thinking skillss including user empathy, system design, behavioral understanding, and the ability to turn real world problems into structured solutions.
